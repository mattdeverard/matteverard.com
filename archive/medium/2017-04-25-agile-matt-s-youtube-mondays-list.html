Agile Matt’s YouTube Mondays List

By Matt Everard · April 25, 2017

“The internet is good in India,” I was told many times. When arriving I learned that “good” was meant in comparison to what they had a decade ago. Bit rates are good enough to stream video, but most locals buy a bandwidth-limited package. And it’s not cheap. We bought a 50GB package our first month for a $100 USD. It was used up in 3 days. Four of us, eight devices. Poof!

So take it for granted that your teammates don’t spend evenings burning bits on youtube. Do them a favor and show them some the interweb’s best on the company dime

I chose to kick-start the week with a fun learning video. Here’s a short list. I showed these just before lunch each Monday to the entire floor. Most are sub-20 minutes, fun, and mentally energizing

  • Hitler in a sprint review (4 mins)
  • Sh*t bad SMs say (3 mins)
  • Agile Product Ownership in a Nutshell — Kniberg (16 mins)
  • Spotify Engineer Culture part 1 & 2 — Kniberg (13 mins each)
  • Humans need not apply (15 mins)
  • JIRA in a nutshell (4 mins)
  • GOTO 2015 • Why Scaling Agile Doesn’t Work • Jez Humble (51 mins — just edit it to include best parts)
  • The art of doing twice as much in half the time | Jeff Sutherland | TEDxAix (18 mins)
  • Living in flow — the secret of happiness with Mihaly Csikszentmihalyi at Happiness & Its Causes 2014 (24 mins)
  • The puzzle of motivation | Dan Pink (18 mins)
  • How great leaders inspire action | Simon Sinek (18 mins)

And just for fun (point and shoot — all amazing)

  • Kurzgesagt — In a Nutshell
  • CGP Grey
