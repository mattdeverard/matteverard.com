Mistakes in Technical Leadership (Hacknot)

Put this site in your "low frequency/high quality" blog list. Hacknot cranks out about one article a month of decent length and excellent quality. Similar in tone and content to Joe Spolsky (cf. the love of private offices). The most recent post details 19 (yes, 19!) mistakes common among tech leads. The first two mistakes help you get a feel:
  1. Assuming the team serves you
  2. Isolating yourself from the team
