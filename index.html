About

Born in Palos Verdes, raised in Columbus. Battelle Scholar at Ohio State (IFYKY) BSEE. My first corporate job was being the IT guy for the C-suite at Huntington National Bank, where I learned that executives want to know two things: when, and how much.

Engineering curriculum left little room for the bigger questions, so I went to grad school in Chicago for theology. Then back to Columbus, partner at a boutique consultancy, survived the dot-com bust, and in 2005 moved the family to Vancouver to raise our two sons near the mountains and ocean.
